Akhil kumar

Born on 3 may 1999 in Mahabubnagar, Telangana,India, Akhil kumar is the son of k.krishnaiah.
His sublime talent was clear from an early age and it was no surprise that he signed on a 2-year county contract with Durham at the age of 18. He made his one-day debut against Surrey in 2009 and marked the occasion by snaring Mark Ramprakash with his 3rd delivery in FC cricket. He played 2 youth Tests against Bangladesh and excelled with both bat and ball. His talent was further noticed in the club cricket where he registered a century . He made his FC debut in the season opener against MCC at Abu Dhabi, scoring his first fifty and picking up a wicket.
